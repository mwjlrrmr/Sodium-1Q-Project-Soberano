Philippines is home to various marine life species. Ranging from small to giant, from black and white to vibrant colors. Let me introduce the pride of the Philippines, the Whale Shark (Rhindocon typus) or most known as Butanding. The Butanding, most spotted at Donsol, Sorsogon and Oslob, Cebu because of the feeding programs that attracts the sharks year-round (Arcadio, n.d.). The Butanding is popular to tourist as you can swim alongside with this cutie.
